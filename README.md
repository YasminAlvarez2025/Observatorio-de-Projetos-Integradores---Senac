## Introduction

The **Observatory of Integrative Projects** is a digital platform developed to centralize, organize, and showcase Integrative Projects created by students at **Senac Pernambuco**.

The platform was designed to address the lack of visibility and long-term use of academic projects after their evaluation process. Its goal is to preserve academic knowledge, streamline project assessment, and enable students to transform their academic work into professional portfolios accessible to professors, coordinators, and industry partners.

---

## Problem Statement

Every semester, higher education institutions generate a significant number of academic projects that represent valuable knowledge, innovation, research, and practical experience.

Despite their potential, many of these projects have a short lifecycle. Once submitted and evaluated, they are often archived, scattered across different systems, or simply forgotten.

As a result:

* Students struggle to showcase their technical skills and academic achievements professionally.
* Professors face difficulties accessing historical project records and tracking project outcomes over time.
* Educational institutions lose opportunities to preserve and leverage the knowledge generated within their academic ecosystem.
* Companies have limited access to emerging talent and innovative solutions developed by students.

This creates a gap between academic production and its long-term impact, reducing opportunities for collaboration, innovation, and professional development.

---

## Solution

The **Observatory of Integrative Projects** provides a centralized platform where:

* Students can submit, manage, and showcase their projects;
* Professors can evaluate projects and provide structured feedback;
* Academic coordinators can monitor institutional indicators and project performance;
* Companies can explore approved public projects and identify talented students.

By connecting academia and industry, the platform transforms academic projects into valuable assets for learning, innovation, and career development.
